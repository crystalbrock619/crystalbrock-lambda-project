Why I am attending Lambda School


I decided to attend Lambda School
after I had to resign in lieu of being fired from my job as a professional truck driver.
I am very creative and I found that driving for a living was
just not satisfying my need to create. This caused me to drive
very agressivley because I just wanted to get the day over with.
As you can imagine, this behavior did not sit well with my employer
and they ended up asking for my resignation.
I had been attempting to learn to code in my spare time,
but found that motivation to practice coding after sitting behind
a stearing wheel for 60 hours a week was in short supply.
So, after a couple of failed attempts to find a new driving job,
I decided I wanted to go to school for web development full time.
My wonderful partner agreed to support our household while I attend school
and that brings us to the present.
This website is for the "Launch a Website" portion of the pre-course
work that Lambda recomends students complete before class starts.
